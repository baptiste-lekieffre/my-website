Create an account

Needel is a social network meant to promote the mode creations of little fashion designers or fashion students. The platform enables to interact with the greatest couture houses or to get visibility by taking part in various fashion contests.

The creator account is restricted to little fashion designers and fashion students. They are the only ones who can publish posts on the application.

The jury account is opened to everyone. Like a real jury, you will rate the different creations submitted for the fashion contests. You can also follow and interact with your favorite creators.
